Artificial Intelligence... ANSWERING ALL THE WHY’S

We the humans are the wisest creature, considered. The quality possessed by us is of uniqueness and this is what makes us the peculiar organism. We have been ruling the solar system as far as we can reach.

An Article by: Megha Singh

The most important thing that keeps us out of the box is the thought process that is generated by our brain.

It’s almost impossible to create a replica of the human mind. But we the Humans, have always been experimenting and the curiosity of it made us make something like Artificial intelligence.

What is artificial intelligence?

Developing computer systems to perform tasks like Visual perception, Speech recognition, Decision making and Translation between the languages.

If we define it in simple terms, we will say making the computers SMART, which is giving them the ability to think and learn without being given the set of instructions and it is the uniqueness of humans that we are trying to embed into the machines.

John McCarthy coined the term Artificial Intelligence in 1955.

Why Artificial Intelligence is important?

Artificial Intelligence adds intelligence to the existing things.

As we think of a problem and get the solution. Similarly AI can go into the deep and analyze more and more data using the neural networks.

Everyone learns from their mistakes and AI uses this technique and is called as progressive learning algorithms.

Getting most from the limited resources is a sign of profit and AI gets the most out of the data as it follows the process of self-learning.

It achieves the incredible accuracy with the help of its neural networks.

Uses of it are boundless, it can be used in a variety of purposes such as exploring spaces, getting incredible speed-accuracy-precision and can perform tasks is the situations where humans cant.
